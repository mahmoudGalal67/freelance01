Future Academy

The Future Academy is the first academy specialized in digital manufacturing in Kuwait. It seeks to create opportunities and prepare and qualify young cadres to manage and establish factories that meet the country's needs, increase local production, and contribute to the development of the industrial sector to achieve self-sufficiency.

This is achieved through theoretical and practical training on the latest advanced technology in the field of digital design and manufacturing to obtain a product that conforms to international quality standards and specifications, using CNC machines with German technology and an operating system from the international company Siemens.

Our Vision

Contributing to the state's efforts to develop the industrial sector by designing, developing and manufacturing spare parts that meet the needs of all sectors of the state with the highest international quality standards and specifications.

Creating an entrepreneurial generation with creative ideas capable of keeping pace with change and development in the industrial field and transforming those ideas into small industrial projects that serve the country's development plan.
